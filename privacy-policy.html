Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 9, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: ToneTrace is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services, and we do not sell your personal information to third parties.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ToneTrace ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use the ToneTrace mobile application ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Search Queries: Text searches you perform for gear identification
  • Photos: Images you upload for photo-based gear identification (processed temporarily, not stored permanently)
  • Favorites and History: Gear items you save as favorites or view in your search history

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, search frequency, time spent in app
  • Subscription Information: Subscription status, purchase history (managed through Apple and RevenueCat)
  • Crash Reports: Technical data when the app crashes to help us fix bugs

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT collect your name, email address, or phone number (unless you contact us for support)
  • We do NOT access your contacts, photo library (except photos you explicitly share), or other personal files
  • We do NOT track your location
  • We do NOT sell your data to third parties

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide Services: To process your searches, identify gear, and deliver search results
  • Personalization: To save your favorites and search history for easy access
  • Subscription Management: To manage your subscription status and entitlements
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, fix bugs, and improve features
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical support
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. Photo Processing and Storage

Important: When you upload a photo for gear identification:

  • Photos are transmitted securely to our servers for processing
  • Photos are analyzed using machine learning algorithms to identify gear
  • Photos are NOT permanently stored unless you explicitly save results to your favorites
  • Photos are automatically deleted from our servers after processing (typically within 24 hours)
  • Cached photos on your device are managed by iOS and can be cleared by deleting the app

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect and process your information:

5.1 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

  • Purpose: Manage subscriptions, verify purchases, and track subscription status
  • Data Shared: Anonymous user ID, subscription status, purchase history
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5.2 Apple App Store

  • Purpose: Process in-app purchases and subscriptions
  • Data Shared: Apple ID, payment information (handled entirely by Apple)
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

5.3 Cloud Service Providers

  • Purpose: Host our servers and process gear identification requests
  • Data Shared: Search queries, photos (temporarily), usage data
  • All data is encrypted in transit and at rest

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services:

  • Search History & Favorites: Stored locally on your device and in cloud backup (if enabled) until you delete the app or clear data
  • Subscription Data: Retained as long as your subscription is active, plus the period required for tax and legal compliance (typically 7 years)
  • Photos: Deleted within 24 hours after processing
  • Usage Analytics: Aggregated and anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for statistical purposes

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption of data at rest on our servers
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability testing
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

8.1 Access and Correction

You can access and update your favorites and search history directly within the App.

8.2 Data Deletion

To delete your data:

  • Delete favorites and search history from within the App
  • Uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data
  • Contact us at eflotty@gmail.com to request deletion of server-side data

8.3 Data Portability

You can export your favorites and search history by contacting us at eflotty@gmail.com.

8.4 Opt-Out

You can opt out of analytics tracking by contacting us. Note that this may limit certain features of the App.

8.5 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

8.6 European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right of access to your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

9. Children's Privacy

ToneTrace is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately, and we will delete such information.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from your country's laws. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

  • Posting the updated policy in the App
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top

Your continued use of the App after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
